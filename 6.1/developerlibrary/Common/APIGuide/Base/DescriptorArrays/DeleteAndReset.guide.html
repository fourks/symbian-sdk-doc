Deleting elements from the arrays

Descriptor arrays provide a means of:

  • deleting chosen elements using Delete()

  • deleting all elements using Reset().

The effect of these functions depends on the type of descriptor array.

For arrays of non-modifiable pointer descriptors, i.e. CPtrCArray types, deleting and resetting removes the non-modifiable pointer descriptors from the array but does not delete the data or descriptors that they point to.

For arrays of pointers, i.e. CDesCArrayFlat and CDesCArraySeg types, deleting and resetting removes the pointers from the array and also deletes the heap descriptors that they point to.
