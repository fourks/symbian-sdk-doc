Enum TGulAdjacent

TGulAdjacent

Support

Supported from 6.0

Description

Enumerates the sides on which borders can be adjacent.

EGulAdjNone

Indicates that rectangle borders are not adjacent.

EGulAdjTop

Adjacent at the top.

EGulAdjBottom

Adjacent at the bottom.

EGulAdjLeft

Adjacent at the left.

EGulAdjRight

Adjacent at the right.
