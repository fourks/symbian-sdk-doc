Class TArithmeticalDate

TArithmeticalDate

Support

Supported from 6.1

Description

Arithmetically-based date.

Used by the Gregorian calendar. Its public member data is the year, month and day.

Defined in TArithmeticalDate:
TArithmeticalDate(), iDay, iMonth, iYear, operator==()
